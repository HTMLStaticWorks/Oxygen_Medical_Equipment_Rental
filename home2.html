Sterile & Safe

Our 5-Stage Clinical Sanitization Protocol

Your safety is our absolute priority. Before any oxygen concentrator, hospital bed, or patient monitor reaches a home, it goes through a rigorous clinical disinfection cycle in our specialized lab.

1

Chemical Deep Clean

Removal of surface impurities and clinical washing using medical-grade antiviral agents.

2

UV-C Chamber Sterilization

Exposure to high-intensity germicidal UV-C light to destroy 99.9% of bacteria and viruses.

3

Biomedical Calibration

Calibrating flow rates, purity indicators, and alarms under strict diagnostic parameters.

4

Clinical Vacuum Wrapping

Sealing the device in airtight dustproof wraps, opened only in your home by our team.
