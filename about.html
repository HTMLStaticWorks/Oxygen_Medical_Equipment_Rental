About Our Service

Dedicated to delivering clean, reliable, and clinical-grade medical equipment directly to families and institutions in their hours of need.

Our Story

Caring for Families & Patients Since 2015

Founded with a simple mission to make life-saving medical equipment accessible and affordable, OxyMed has grown to become the most trusted healthcare logistics service in the state.

We understand the immense stress that comes with home care. Whether you are assisting a recovery or managing a chronic condition, we treat your requests with the highest urgency, delivering quality equipment fully sterilized, set up, and ready to protect your loved ones.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between quality medical facilities and home healthcare by providing affordable, clinical-grade equipment rentals with exceptional support and logistics speed.

Our Vision

To be the leading national provider of home care respiratory and mobility equipment rentals, recognized for medical integrity, sanitation standards, and compassionate assistance.
